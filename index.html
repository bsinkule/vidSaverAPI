vidSaver

vidSaver lets you save YouTube videos to your device without the need to have an account. Just paste a YouTube URL at the top, click "add vid" and your video will be added to the page. When you finish watching your videos, you can click "clear all", all of the videos will be removed and you can load it back up again with more videos!

If you click "random vid" a random YouTube video will be loaded for you to watch. The random videos will not be saved to your device, so when you refresh, you will still have your saved videos and the random videos will be deleted.
